Our End-to-End Process

We follow a proven methodology to take you from data chaos to data clarity and AI-readiness.

1

Discovery & Strategy

We start by understanding your business goals. We identify your key data sources, map out your most critical business questions, and define a clear data-driven strategy and roadmap.

2

Data Foundation

We build your central source of truth. This includes setting up a scalable cloud data warehouse, creating robust pipelines to pull data from all your sources (APIs, databases, files), and modeling the data for performance and reliability.

3

BI & Dashboards

With the foundation in place, we build the essential executive and departmental dashboards that give you a 360-degree view of your business performance, tracking your most important KPIs.

4

AI Enablement

This is where you leapfrog the competition. We connect your new data warehouse to our automated cataloging agent and deploy the WizBI AI Analysts, empowering your entire team with self-service insights.

5

Handover & Partnership

We don't just build and leave. We provide full documentation, training for your team, and can offer ongoing support to ensure your data ecosystem grows with your business.

Full-Service Project FAQs

Your questions about our end-to-end partnership, answered.

The timeline varies based on complexity (e.g., the number of data sources), but our agile approach delivers value fast. A foundational data warehouse and initial set of dashboards can often be delivered within **6-8 weeks**. The full AI enablement and team-wide rollout follows shortly after. We prioritize getting you actionable insights as quickly as possible.

We build on a modern, best-in-class data stack that is scalable and cost-effective. While we are technology-agnostic and can adapt to your needs, a typical stack includes:
• Data Warehouse: Google BigQuery or Snowflake.
• Data Pipelines/ETL: Fivetran, Airbyte, or custom-built pipelines.
• Data Modeling: dbt (Data Build Tool).
• BI & AI Layer: Our proprietary BI MAGICS platform (Agent & WizBI).
We choose the right tools for the job to ensure you own a future-proof, enterprise-grade infrastructure.
