How The Magic Happens

Our Agent follows a four-step process to build the intelligent foundation for WizBI.

1. Secure Connection

It starts with a simple, secure, read-only connection to your data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, etc.). This takes minutes and can be scoped to specific schemas.

2. Automated Cataloging

Our Agent scans metadata (e.g., `INFORMATION_SCHEMA`) to discover every table, view, and column, creating a living data catalog without any manual effort from your team.

3. Contextual Understanding

The Agent analyzes query logs to understand relationships, join keys, and popular query patterns. It then uses AI to generate human-readable documentation and summaries for your tables.

4. Powering WizBI

This rich, automated context is the "brain" behind WizBI. It allows the AI Analyst to understand business terms, translate questions into efficient SQL, and deliver accurate answers to your users.
