Your Questions, Answered

Here are some common questions about how we make the magic happen.

BI MAGICS is a unified data platform designed to eliminate the complexity between your raw data and the people who need answers. We do this in two steps:
1. Our automated backend connects to your data warehouse, learns your data's structure, and prepares it for analysis.
2. We provide your business users with **WizBI**, a simple chat interface where they can ask questions in plain English and get immediate, reliable answers, charts, and insights. It's the end of dashboard bottlenecks.

Traditional BI tools like Looker and Tableau are powerful but require data experts to manually model data and build every single dashboard and report. This creates a bottleneck where business users have to wait days or weeks for new questions to be answered.

BI MAGICS flips the model. We automate the data modeling and preparation, and instead of relying on pre-built dashboards, we empower users to ask any question on their own, whenever they want. This provides true self-service analytics and frees up your data team to focus on more strategic projects.
