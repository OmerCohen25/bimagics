BI Magics

A standalone platform bringing business intelligence to the masses

Why Now?

Giant companies like Google and Microsoft are launching advanced BI systems with AI integrations — all aimed at large organizations with BI teams and sophisticated models. BI Magics aims to make business intelligence accessible, simple, and immediate — for every team, small business, or entrepreneur. No heavy infrastructure, no technical knowledge, and no waiting for an analyst.

5 Revolutionary Products

1. ChatBI for Imperfect Data

What it is: An intelligent AI chat that interacts with Google Sheets, CSVs, or messy tables.

What it can do: Understand business intent, fix broken columns, build queries from scratch, and provide accurate insights without any prior knowledge.

2. Smart Feed

What it is: A live feed of BI cards showing new insights, anomalies, and action recommendations.

How it works: The feed updates automatically when there's a significant data change or when a question is asked. Everything is presented clearly — like a social network for your business.

3. Custom Agents

What it is: Smart BI assistants for every employee or team, easily built by the user themselves.

Possible uses: "Finance Analyst", "Inventory Guardian", "Customer Agent" — each Agent with straightforward settings and tailored responses based on their area of interest.

4. Dashboard Studio

What it is: A smart dashboard creation environment — in chat, UI, or code (JSON).

Includes: Support for drag & drop, direct editing, GitHub publishing, dynamic URL sharing, View/Code displays.

5. Data Marketplace & Connectors

What it is: Easy connections to sources like BigQuery, Google Sheets, Salesforce, and more — as well as public databases.

Planned additions: Ability to share Agents and dashboards, profit from them (Marketplace), and build community BI templates.

3 Main Audiences

1. Product and Ops Teams

Problem: No time to wait for data, no one to build reports. They ask many questions in chat.

BI Magics provides: Instant insights for every question, Feed with action recommendations, and complete autonomy.

2. Small and Medium Businesses

Problem: No BI team, just Excel files and confusion. Unable to pay for a heavy solution.

BI Magics provides: "BI without BI" — chat, data, insights, with no need for any knowledge.

3. BI Leads in Large Organizations

Problem: Flooded with repetitive requests; users keep asking the same question every week.

BI Magics provides: Creation of Agents based on frequently asked questions, enormous time savings, and true self-service.
