Built for Professionals

Addressing the key questions for data, security, and engineering teams.

Security is foundational to our architecture. We operate on a **read-only** basis and **never store a copy of your raw data**.

Our platform can be deployed within your **VPC**, ensuring your data never leaves your secure environment. Access controls are granular, allowing you to manage permissions at the column level, and all connections are encrypted end-to-end. We primarily analyze metadata to build our catalog, minimizing our interaction with sensitive data itself.

Yes, our platform is designed for scale. We don't ingest or process your data ourselves. Instead, we generate optimized SQL queries that run **natively inside your data warehouse** (e.g., Snowflake, BigQuery). This leverages the immense processing power you already have and ensures we never become a performance bottleneck. Our metadata-first approach is lightweight and efficient, even on terabyte- or petabyte-scale environments.

Integration is fast and straightforward. On the backend, we have native, read-only connectors for all major cloud data warehouses. On the frontend, we meet users where they are. Our AI Analyst is available via **Slack**, **WhatsApp**, and a clean **web application**, ensuring seamless adoption into your existing workflows with minimal disruption.

BI MAGICS is a force multiplier for your data team, not a replacement. By automating the data catalog and handling the flood of ad-hoc reporting requests from business users, we free up your data experts to focus on what they do best: building robust data models, working on complex strategic analysis, and driving core data architecture. Your data team remains in full control of governance and security, while business users get the autonomy they crave.
