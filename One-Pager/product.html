Sample Question:

"Analyze the adoption of our new 'dashboard' feature launched in Q1. Segment by user type (new vs. returning) and identify drop-off points in the activation funnel."

Analyzing user behavior... building funnels...

ðŸ’¡ Product Insight:

  • High Adoption by New Users: 72% of new users completed feature activation, compared to only 31% of returning users.
  • Critical Drop-off Point: 45% of users drop off at the "Add First Widget" step. Returning users rarely reach this stage.
  • Hypothesis: Returning users don't see the immediate value, or the feature is not prominent enough in the existing UI.
  • Recommendation: Run an A/B test with a more prominent tooltip for returning users and add pre-built widget templates to simplify onboarding.
