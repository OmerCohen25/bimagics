Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try the space for a day?

Absolutely! Our Flex Desk (Day Pass) is the perfect way to experience SproutSpace. You can book one directly from our contact page.

What are the opening hours?

Our community manager is on-site from 9:00 to 18:00, Sunday to Thursday. Resident Desk and Private Studio members have 24/7 access.

Is there parking available?

While we don't have dedicated parking, there are several paid parking lots nearby. We highly encourage using public transport or bicycles!
